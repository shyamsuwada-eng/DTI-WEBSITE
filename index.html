About ECE Department

The ECE Department at Lendi Institute of Engineering and Technology is dedicated to providing a solid foundation in electronics and communication engineering. Our comprehensive curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. We regularly conduct symposiums, workshops, and academic events to enhance students' technical aptitude and leadership qualities.

Online courses, seminars, and webinars led by industry experts ensure our students stay updated with the latest technological advancements. Through technical assessments, mock interviews, and career guidance, we prepare students for successful careers. Over the past seven years, our graduates have secured positions in top MNCs, achieving impressive salary packages.

At Lendi's ECE Department, student growth and knowledge acquisition are paramount.
